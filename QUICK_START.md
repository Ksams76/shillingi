## 🏃 Get Started in 2 Minutes

### Step 1: Open the App
- Open `index.html` in your web browser
- That's it! No installation needed.

### Step 2: Log Your First Transaction
1. Click **"Record Transaction"** section
2. Select: **Income** (money coming in) or **Expense** (money going out)
3. Write what you sold/bought (e.g., "2kg tomatoes")
4. Enter the amount in KES (shillings)
5. Click **"Add Transaction"**

### Step 3: Check Your Dashboard
- Look at the right side to see:
  - **Total Income** - All money you made
  - **Total Expenses** - All money you spent
  - **Net Profit** - Your actual earnings
  - **Charts** - Visual breakdown

**That's it!** Your data is automatically saved.

## 🔍 Understanding Your Dashboard

### Key Numbers Explained

**Total Income** 💚  
= All money customers paid you

**Total Expenses** ❤️  
= All money you spent on stock/supplies

**Net Profit** 💜  
= Income minus Expenses = YOUR EARNINGS

**Profit Margin %**  
= How much profit you made per shilling earned
- Example: 40% = For every 100 KES earned, you keep 40 KES

**Avg Daily Revenue**  
= Average money you made each day

## 💾 Keeping Your Data Safe

### Your Data is 100% Private
- Stored only on your device
- Not sent to any server

1. Go to Dashboard section

## 📞 Need Help?

### Check these first:
1. Make sure JavaScript is enabled in your browser
2. Try refreshing the page (F5)
3. Try a different browser if it doesn't work
4. Make sure you fill in all required fields (*)

### Common Issues:

**"Nothing saves"**
- Check if browser allows LocalStorage (usually enabled by default)
- Try clearing browser cache and cookies
- Try a different browser

**"Charts not showing"**
- Need at least one transaction to see charts
- Refresh the page
- Check browser console (F12) for errors

**"Data disappeared"**
- Check if you cleared browser data/cache
- Use your exported JSON backup to restore
- Export data monthly to prevent loss
